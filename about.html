Our Story

A Trusted Name in Faisalabad's Plumbing Trade

Jalandhar Pipe Store was established in 2003 in the heart of Faisalabad, Pakistan — one of the country's most vibrant industrial cities. What began as a small neighborhood hardware shop has grown over two decades into a comprehensive plumbing materials supplier serving hundreds of customers every month.

We stock a wide range of products including PVC and UPVC pipes, ball and gate valves, pipe fittings of all types and sizes, water storage tanks, submersible pumps, bathroom accessories and all the tools and materials a plumber or contractor needs for any project — big or small.

Our team's expertise and commitment to stocking only genuine, branded products from trusted manufacturers like Adamjee, Faisal Pipes, and Dura Max has earned us the trust of the local plumbing community. We serve professional plumbers, civil contractors, builders and homeowners alike.
