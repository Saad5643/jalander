Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer bulk/wholesale pricing for contractors?
Yes! We offer special bulk pricing for contractors, builders and plumbers who purchase large quantities. Contact us on WhatsApp or visit the store with your requirements for a custom quote.
Which pipe brands do you stock?
We carry Adamjee, Faisal Pipes, Dura Max, Master Pipes, and Pak Pipes — all trusted brands widely used by Pakistan's plumbing professionals.
Can I place an order on WhatsApp?
Absolutely! Message us your list of required products on WhatsApp (041 2645043) and we'll confirm availability and pricing. You can then collect from the store at your convenience.
Do you help with pipe sizing and fitting selection?
Yes, our experienced staff is happy to guide you. Whether you need advice on pipe sizing, valve selection, or fitting types — describe your project and we'll recommend the right materials.
What are your store hours?
We are open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. On Fridays we close for Juma prayer from 1:00 PM to 3:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.
